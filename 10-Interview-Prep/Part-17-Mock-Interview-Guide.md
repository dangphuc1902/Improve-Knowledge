# PART 17 - MOCK INTERVIEW GUIDE

> **Instructions**: This guide prepares you for the live mock interview.
> The actual mock interview is conducted interactively — see the SRS folder below.

---

## How to Use This Mock Interview System

### Interview Flow
1. Start a fresh conversation/session
2. Tell the AI: *"Act as a Senior Java Backend interviewer. Ask me one question at a time and wait for my answer before continuing."*
3. Answer each question out loud (practice speaking)
4. Request feedback on: technical accuracy, English, confidence

### Evaluation Rubric (Interviewer uses this)

| Dimension | 1 (Poor) | 3 (Average) | 5 (Excellent) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Technical Accuracy | Wrong/missing key points | Partially correct | Complete and correct |
| English Grammar | Many errors | Occasional errors | Fluent and natural |
| Structure | Rambling, no order | Some structure | Clear STAR/logical flow |
| Depth | Surface-level | Adequate | Shows deep understanding |
| Confidence | Hesitant, long pauses | Moderate | Confident, clear delivery |

## Mock Interview Question Bank (20 Questions)

### Round 1: Introduction & HR (5 min)
1. Tell me about yourself.
2. Why are you interested in this position?

### Round 2: Core Java (10 min)
3. What is the difference between HashMap and ConcurrentHashMap?
4. Explain how the JVM garbage collector works.
5. What is the difference between `==` and `.equals()` in Java?

### Round 3: Spring (10 min)
6. Explain Spring Dependency Injection and its types.
7. What happens when a `@Transactional` method calls another `@Transactional` method?
8. What is Spring AOP and how does it work internally?

### Round 4: Database (10 min)
9. What is the N+1 problem in Hibernate and how do you solve it?
10. Explain the difference between a clustered and non-clustered index.
11. Write a SQL query to find the second highest salary per department.

### Round 5: Microservices (10 min)
12. How does a Circuit Breaker pattern work?
13. What is the difference between Choreography and Orchestration in Saga pattern?
14. How do you handle distributed transactions in microservices?

### Round 6: System Design (15 min)
15. Design a URL shortener for 1 billion URLs. Walk me through your approach.

### Round 7: Behavioral (10 min)
16. Describe a time you had to make a difficult technical decision under time pressure.
17. Tell me about a production incident you handled.

### Round 8: English Communication (5 min)
18. How do you explain a technical delay to a non-technical client?
19. A client is upset that a feature wasn't delivered on time. How do you respond?
20. Do you have any questions for me?

## Common Interview Mistakes to Avoid

### Technical
- ❌ Saying "I think it works like..." without conviction
- ❌ Getting confused between HashMap and Hashtable basics
- ❌ Not mentioning trade-offs (everything has pros and cons)
- ❌ Skipping error handling in code examples
- ❌ Not knowing time/space complexity of your solutions

### Communication
- ❌ Answering too quickly without structuring thoughts
- ❌ Using "umm", "err", "like" excessively
- ❌ Not asking for clarification when question is unclear
- ❌ Giving yes/no answers without elaboration
- ❌ Not having questions to ask the interviewer

### Behavioral
- ❌ Criticizing previous employers or colleagues
- ❌ Using "we" without specifying YOUR role
- ❌ Vague answers without specific examples
- ❌ Showing no learning from past failures

## Interview Day Checklist

### 30 Minutes Before
- [ ] Test your internet connection and camera
- [ ] Review your top 3 strengths and 2 weaknesses
- [ ] Have your CV and portfolio ready to reference
- [ ] Prepare 2-3 questions to ask the interviewer
- [ ] Quiet environment, good lighting

### During Interview
- [ ] Greet warmly and professionally
- [ ] Listen fully before answering — don't interrupt
- [ ] Ask for clarification if needed: "Just to make sure I understand..."
- [ ] Think out loud during technical questions
- [ ] Use STAR format for behavioral questions
- [ ] End each answer confidently, don't trail off

### Closing Questions to Ask
1. "What does the typical career path look like for someone in this role?"
2. "What are the biggest technical challenges the team is currently facing?"
3. "How does the team handle code quality and technical debt?"
4. "What does a successful first 90 days look like for this role?"
