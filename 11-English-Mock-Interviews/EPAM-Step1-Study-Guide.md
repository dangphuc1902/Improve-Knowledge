## 5. 3 Câu Chuyện STAR

> 💡 **Công thức STAR:**  
> **S**ituation (Bối cảnh) → **T**ask (Nhiệm vụ) → **A**ction (Hành động) → **R**esult (Kết quả có số liệu)

---

### 📖 Story 1: Giải Quyết Vấn Đề Kỹ Thuật (Performance)

**HR hỏi:**
> *"Tell me about a challenging technical problem you solved."*

**✅ Script STAR:**

> **S:** *"At Gihot Studio, during stress testing at 10,000 virtual concurrent users, we observed significant database CPU spikes and rising latency in the player matchmaking service."*
>
> **T:** *"My responsibility was to identify the root cause and bring latency back within SLA limits — with our product launch deadline approaching."*
>
> **A:** *"I profiled the application systematically. I discovered that active player session data was being fetched from MySQL on every single request — there was no caching at all. I redesigned the architecture: I placed a Redis caching layer in front of the session store, and moved non-critical game events to RabbitMQ async queues so they wouldn't block the main request thread."*
>
> **R:** *"The result: database load dropped by 30%, API response times improved by 20%, and end-to-end latency reduced by 25% under peak load. We launched on schedule."*

**📝 Key phrases trong Story 1:**
| Cụm từ | Nghĩa |
|:---|:---|
| **stress testing** | kiểm tra tải cao |
| **CPU spikes** | CPU tăng đột ngột |
| **SLA limits** | giới hạn mức độ dịch vụ cam kết |
| **I profiled the application** | tôi phân tích hiệu năng ứng dụng |
| **systematically** | có hệ thống, từng bước |
| **async queues** | hàng đợi xử lý bất đồng bộ |

---

### 📖 Story 2: Làm Việc Nhóm / Giải Quyết Xung Đột

**HR hỏi:**
> *"Tell me about a time you had a disagreement with a colleague. How did you handle it?"*

**✅ Script STAR:**

> **S:** *"During a client-server integration at Gihot, the client-side team reported latency issues and attributed them to our backend gRPC endpoints."*
>
> **T:** *"As the backend engineer responsible for that component, I needed to either validate or disprove the claim — without damaging the relationship between the two teams."*
>
> **A:** *"Instead of pushing back defensively, I proposed a joint debugging session. I set up benchmark tests that measured gRPC payload execution time and network delivery time separately. We discovered the real bottleneck: uncompressed Protobuf payloads over HTTP — a shared issue, not a backend-only problem."*
>
> **R:** *"We optimized the Protobuf schema together. Client response time dropped under 50 milliseconds. More importantly, it built strong trust between the frontend and backend teams — and we adopted that same collaborative debugging approach throughout the rest of the project."*

**📝 Key phrases trong Story 2:**
| Cụm từ | Nghĩa |
|:---|:---|
| **attributed to** | cho rằng nguyên nhân là do |
| **validate or disprove** | xác nhận hoặc bác bỏ |
| **pushing back defensively** | phản ứng phòng thủ, bác bỏ ngay lập tức |
| **benchmark tests** | kiểm tra đo điểm chuẩn |
| **collaborative** | hợp tác cùng nhau |

---

### 📖 Story 3: Học Công Nghệ Mới Nhanh

**HR hỏi:**
> *"Tell me about a time you had to learn something new quickly."*

**✅ Script STAR:**

> **S:** *"When I joined Gihot Studio, the tech stack included C++17 for game server logic — a language I had studied but never used in production. The team needed me to be productive within 3 months."*
>
> **T:** *"I needed to learn production-grade C++ concurrency — mutex locks, atomic operations, and memory management — while simultaneously delivering on actual sprint tasks."*
>
> **A:** *"I didn't just read documentation. I built small proof-of-concept programs to validate my understanding of thread safety before touching any production code. I also specifically requested code reviews from senior C++ engineers — focused on correctness and safety, not just functionality."*
>
> **R:** *"Within 3 months, I was shipping production C++ features independently. That experience gave me a deep understanding of low-level concurrency that directly makes my Java code better — I now think naturally about thread safety in ways many Java-only engineers don't."*

**📝 Key phrases trong Story 3:**
| Cụm từ | Nghĩa |
|:---|:---|
| **simultaneously** | đồng thời |
| **proof-of-concept programs** | chương trình thử nghiệm nhỏ để kiểm chứng |
| **thread safety** | an toàn khi chạy đa luồng |
| **correctness** | tính chính xác |
| **shipping features** | hoàn thành và release tính năng |

---

### 🔁 Luyện STAR Stories
> Với mỗi story:
> 1. Đọc to 2 lần có nhìn
> 2. Nói lại **chỉ dùng các keyword** (S/T/A/R)
> 3. Tự kể từ đầu không nhìn — giới hạn 2 phút mỗi story

## 9. Xử Lý Tình Huống Khó

### 🔴 Khi không hiểu câu hỏi:
> *"Could you please rephrase that? I want to make sure I understand the question correctly."*  
> *"Just to clarify — are you asking about [A] or [B]?"*

---

### 🔴 Khi cần thêm thời gian suy nghĩ:
> *"That's a great question. Let me take a moment to structure my thoughts."*  
> *"Let me think about that for a second — I want to give you a precise answer."*

---

### 🔴 Khi chưa có kinh nghiệm thực tế về thứ gì đó:
> *"I haven't used that in production yet, but based on my experience with [related technology], I understand the core concept. I'm confident I can get up to speed quickly."*
>
> *"I've studied [topic] and applied it in my personal project. It's definitely something I'm actively building toward in my next role."*

---

### 🔴 Khi cần sửa lại câu vừa nói:
> *"Let me rephrase that — what I meant was..."*  
> *"Actually, I'd like to add one more point to that..."*

---

### 🔴 KHÔNG BAO GIỜ nói:
| ❌ Nói | ✅ Thay bằng |
|:---|:---|
| *"My English is not very good"* | Cứ nói chậm và rõ — đừng xin lỗi |
| *"I don't know"* | *"I haven't worked with that yet, but..."* |
| *"We did X"* | *"I implemented X / I was responsible for X"* |
| *"I'm leaving because of salary"* | *"I'm looking for a larger engineering challenge"* |
