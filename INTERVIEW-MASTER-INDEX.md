## 🎯 Interview Day Survival Kit

### Top 5 Questions You MUST Answer Perfectly
1. **"Tell me about yourself"** — Rehearse until smooth, 90 seconds
2. **"How does HashMap work?"** — Know internals, complexity, thread safety
3. **"Explain @Transactional propagation"** — REQUIRED, REQUIRES_NEW, self-invocation
4. **"What is the N+1 problem?"** — Define, detect, fix with JOIN FETCH
5. **"Design a URL shortener"** — Practice the 5-minute walkthrough

### Emergency Recovery Phrases
- Don't know: *"That's not my strongest area, but here's how I'd reason through it..."*
- Made mistake: *"Let me correct myself — what I meant is..."*
- Need time: *"Let me think through that for a moment..."*
- Unclear question: *"Could you clarify — are you asking about X or Y?"*

## 🏆 Success Metrics

You're ready for the interview when:
- [ ] You can introduce yourself in 90 seconds without notes
- [ ] You can explain HashMap, GC, and @Transactional clearly in English
- [ ] You can design a URL shortener system in 5 minutes
- [ ] You can solve Easy LeetCode in < 5 minutes, Medium in < 15 minutes
- [ ] You have 3-5 specific STAR stories ready
- [ ] You have 3-5 questions to ask the interviewer
- [ ] You've completed at least 2 full mock interviews
